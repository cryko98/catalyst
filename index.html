Ask it about any coin and it pulls the live numbers from on-chain data — price, market cap, liquidity, volume — then gives you a straight, no-fluff read of the risks.

Every fifteen minutes it transmits a market thought of its own. Any time, it will answer yours. It is not a financial advisor and will never tell you what to buy — only what the data says and where the risk sits.
